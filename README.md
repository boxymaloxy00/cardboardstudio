"CardboardStudio" is based off Studio Lite, built for intermediate to advanced Roblox scripters who do not have a computer on hand.
CardboardStudio is built for efficiency and freedom, With the inclusion of modulescripts, Custom publishers, And more to be suggested by the community.
We are not trying to copy Studio Lite. Only trying to modernize the parts where Studio Lite was outdated while carrying an entirely new essence.

Our goal is to let mobile players do and make more,
Regardless of if they have a computer or not.

--
Any games whose sole purpose is trying to expand on this framework and/or the whole game must state that
they are CardboardStudio-based in the description, and credit ScottSpiritWalker for making Studio Lite.

but do not credit any of the development team of CardboardStudio, ScottSpiritWalker deserves all the credit.
--

 
It is currently in conceptualization, and the creator himself will soon soft-launch with some of the core features.
We are only open-sourcing important scripts.
